Moreover, rather than pouring unnecessary capital into what can be considered a failed PS5, it would be better to make fundamental improvements with the PS6. Thus, it is reasonable to scale back tactics to a level that won't disappoint investors, as long as there are profits, criticism will be avoided. </blockquote><blockquote>这种观点的背景可能是，索尼集团设想的 PS5 压倒 Xbox 的目标现在看来已无法实现。此外，与其将不必要的资金投入到可以说是失败的 PS5 上，还不如从根本上改进 PS6。因此，将战术缩减到一个不会让投资者失望的水平是合理的，只要有利润，批评就会避免。</blockquote>
