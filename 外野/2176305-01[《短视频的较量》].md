Dignity contained in 30 centimeters

Chasy Derek

30厘米的尊严

恰西.德里克

Wang Husheng, a staff member of the Shanghai government department, had a flat tire in his car that day, and he had no choice but to walk to work because he did not catch the bus.

王沪生是上海政府部门的工作人员，那天，他的轿车轮胎爆了，又没有赶上公交车，只好选择步行去上班。

Walking through the familiar street, he saw an old man in rags, rummaging through the garbage can. The dumpster was too tall for the old man, and he stood on tiptoe, barely missing his upper body in the dumpster. After a while, the old man found two bottles of mineral water and put them in the bag he carried with him. His face was stained with dirt, but he didn't bother to wipe it, and ran to another bin.

走过熟悉的街口，他看到一个衣衫褴褛的老者，正趴在垃圾箱上翻找着什么。垃圾箱对老人来说太高了，他踮起脚尖，上半身几乎都没在了垃圾箱里。过了一会儿，老人找出两个矿泉水瓶，放到了随身的袋子里。他的脸上沾满了脏东西，可是他顾不上擦，又奔向了另一个垃圾箱。

Wang had never paid attention to these scavengers struggling at the bottom of society before, and looking at the scene in front of him, he felt sad. Turning another street, the king saw a topless teenager. The teenager is as tall as the dumpster, he almost hangs from the dumpster, and Chris is worried that the teenager will fall into it. The boy tinkered for a long time, but he still found nothing, but he didn't give up and simply climbed into the garbage can. A few minutes later, the boy popped up with a few more white bottles in his hands. He found that there were scavengers on almost every street, including the elderly, children, and women, all of whom were small and some with disabilities, and that the garbage bins that stood on the streets were like mountains waiting to be conquered王以前从没留心过这些挣扎在社会底层的拾荒者，看着眼前的场景，他一阵心酸。再转过一条街，王看到了一个裸着上身的少年。少年和垃圾箱一样高，他几乎悬挂在垃圾箱上，克里斯很担心少年会掉进去。少年鼓捣了半天，还是一无所获，但是他不放弃，索性爬进了垃圾箱。几分钟后，少年冒出头来，他的手里多了几个白色的饮料瓶。他发现，几乎每条街都有拾荒者，有老人、儿童、妇女，他们个子都很矮小，有的还有残疾，耸立街头的垃圾箱就像一座座高山等待他们去征服。

The next day, Wang wrote a proposal to renovate the city's garbage bins and handed it to the superior in charge of urban planning, he wrote: In order to help the people living at the bottom of our city, the government has taken many measures, such as distributing daily necessities and providing the most basic living security, but I believe that welfare should not be just a superficial rescue action, but should let some people who voluntarily support themselves get the dignity they deserve. I checked that the bins on several main streets in Shanghai could be 1.5 meters high, and I suggested lowering the height of all the bins by 30 centimeters, so that the waste pickers would not have to be disgraced in order to get a bottle.

第二天，王写出了一份关于改造城市垃圾箱的提案交到了主管城市规划的上级手里，他这样写道：为了帮助生活在我们这座城市底层的人们，政府已经采取了很多措施，彼如发放生活必需品，提供最基本的生活保障金，但是我认为，搞福利不应该单单是表面的救助行动，而是应该让一些自愿自食其力的人得到应有的尊严。我考查了一下，在上海几条主街上的垃圾箱能有1.5米高，我建议把所有的垃圾箱的高度都降低30公厘米，这样拾荒者为了得到一个瓶子就不用灰头土脸了。

Wang Husheng's humane proposal was quickly approved by his superiors. A month later, a garbage bin with a height of 30 centimeters appeared on the streets of Shanghai. Not only has the bin been shrunk, but the bin can also be flipped over, so that the scavengers can easily pick up the "treasures" in the bin.

王沪生充满人情味的提案很快得到了上级的批复。一个月后，身高被压缩了30厘米的垃圾箱出现在了上海街头。设计者别出心裁，不仅仅缩小了垃圾箱，而且垃圾箱还可以翻转，这样拾荒者很方便就能捡到垃圾箱里的“宝贝”了。

The seemingly small move of the Shanghai government has touched countless people, and some people said on social networking sites: this first initiator has a noble personality and deserves everyone's respect; Some people also said: 30 centimeters may be very short and insignificant, but from this shortened 30 centimeters, we see the government's humane care for the disadvantaged, and this 30 centimeters distance allows the weak to see the dignity that they cannot be ignored.

上海政府看似小小的举动却感动了无数人，有人在社交网站上说：这个最先倡议者人格高尚，值得每一个人去尊敬；还有人说： 30厘米也许很短，微不足道，可是从这缩短的30厘米里，我们看到了政府对弱势群体的人性关怀，这30厘米的距离让弱小者看到了自己不能被忽视的尊严。
